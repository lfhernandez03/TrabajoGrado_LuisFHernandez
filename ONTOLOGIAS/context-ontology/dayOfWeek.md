# dayOfWeek

## Metadata

- **Tipo:** Propiedad de Dato (`owl:DatatypeProperty`)
- **Ontologia:** context-ontology
- **Etiqueta:** day of week

## Descripcion

Day of the week when the request is made (Monday, Tuesday, etc.). Neutral temporal information that allows the LLM to make contextual inferences without inducing bias. For example, detecting 'it's Tuesday night' without forcing categories like 'weekday'.

# desiredEnergyLevel

## Metadata

- **Tipo:** Propiedad de Dato (`owl:DatatypeProperty`)
- **Ontologia:** context-ontology
- **Etiqueta:** desired energy level

## Descripcion

Desired energy or intensity level for the movie, separate from the current emotional state. Typical values: 'high', 'medium', 'low', 'relaxing', 'intense'. Allows expressing: 'I'm sad but want something energetic to cheer me up' vs 'I'm sad and want something calm'. Separates how I feel (mood) from the dynamic I want (energy).
